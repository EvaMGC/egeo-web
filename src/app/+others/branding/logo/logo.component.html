Logo

The STRATIO logotype is made up of both a graphic symbol or isotype and the STRATIO type. Together they create the imagetype to form the complete brand.

These two elements should appear as shown here in all corporate materials including the STRATIO platform, website, and collateral materials.

Sometimes we may include the concept line but it is very important to respect the logo proportions and the minimum sizes.

The isotype may appear separately in special situations such as on promotional items or minimum sizes. The corporate configuration is the preferred usage however.

Isotype

Sometimes the logo is reduced there is a point at which it becomes ineffective. By establishing a minimum size for the logo it is ensured the logo is always prominent and readable.

The STRATIO logo can be reduced in printing up to 60 mm (base), while it can be raster scanned (72 dpi) up to 180 pixels.

If a further reduction is required, it is advisable to use the optional logo made to support further reductions and keep the official logo recognizable and unaltered.
